About

Nike, Inc. is a global leader in the design, marketing, and distribution of athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment. Founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, Nike has grown into one of the most recognizable and valuable brands in the world. The company's innovative products and marketing strategies have made it a favorite among athletes and fashion enthusiasts alike. Nike is renowned for its commitment to innovation, constantly pushing the boundaries of athletic performance through cutting-edge technology and design. The iconic Nike Swoosh logo and the "Just Do It" slogan are symbols of the brand's dedication to inspiring and empowering athletes to reach their full potential. With a wide range of products catering to various sports and lifestyle needs, Nike continues to lead the industry by setting trends and driving the future of sportswear. The company also prioritizes sustainability, aiming to reduce its environmental impact through eco-friendly practices and materials. Explore the world of Nike and discover how we're making sport a daily habit, one step at a time.
